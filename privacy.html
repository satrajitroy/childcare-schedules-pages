ChildCare Schedules Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

ChildCare Schedules is designed as an offline, on-device scheduling app.

Data entered into the app

Users may enter information such as staff names, classroom names, schedule profiles, availability, staffing rules, child-count estimates, and generated schedules.

The app is not intended for storing sensitive child records, medical records, payroll records, or confidential personnel files.

Local storage

Schedule profiles and generated scheduling data are stored locally on the user’s device.

ChildCare Schedules does not require an account, does not use a scheduling server, and does not send schedule data to the developer.

Network use

ChildCare Schedules does not require a network connection for scheduling. The app does not use analytics, advertising, remote configuration, or cloud sync.

Data leaves the device only when the user chooses to export or share a schedule, profile, PDF, CSV, JSON file, or other app-generated file using iOS sharing features. Those sharing actions are controlled by the user and by the destination service selected by the user.

Data collection

The developer does not collect personal data through the app.

The app does not track users across apps or websites.

The app does not sell user data.

Third-party services

ChildCare Schedules does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

User control

Users can delete locally stored schedule profiles and generated files from the app or from their device storage where applicable.

Children’s privacy

ChildCare Schedules is intended for childcare schedule planning by adults. It is not intended for use by children and does not require child names or child personal records.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s data practices change. The updated policy will be posted at the app’s privacy policy URL.
